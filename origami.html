Interesting facts about chameleons

  • Chameleons are animals that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is a important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts about pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navagational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about camels

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to kepp sand out of their eyes.
  • There are two types of camels one with one hump on it's back and one with two humps on it's back.
  • Camels can grow up to 7 feet tall and weigh up to 1500 pounds.

Interesting facts about pandas

  • Pandas have sixth fingers on each paw to eat bamboo more efficiently.
  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment.
  • An adult panda can eat up to 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day.

Interesting facts about cicadas

  • Cicadas spend 13 or 17 years underground and then a few weeks above ground until they die.
  • The loud buzzing noise you hear outside is a chorus of male cicadas.
  • Cicadas are actually the loudest insect in the world.

Interesting facts about bears

  • Bears are extraordinary intelligent animals.
  • There is a species of bear called sun bears that are the smallest bears in the world.
  • Polar bears are the largest species of bear.
